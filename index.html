Hi, I completed my doctorate degree from Harbin Engineering University in Dec. 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Chunguang Ma.

I have been very fortunate to have worked with two great researchers during my graduate studies, such as Prof. Steven Galbraith and Prof. Hong-Sheng Zhou. From Sep. 2016 - Sep. 2017, I was a visiting Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Virginia Commonwealth University, under the direction of Prof. Hong-Sheng Zhou. From Dec. 2015 - June. 2016, I was a visiting Ph.D. student in Mathematics Department at the University of Auckland, under the direction of Prof. Steven Galbraith.

Now, I am looking for a position with a University where I can stay and grow with.

Research Interests: My primary research interests are in cryptography and secure distributed computing, including lattice-based cryptography, multiparty computation, and blockchain consensus. Recently, specific topics include: compute and query on encrypted data, verify computation in outsourced environments, various settings of multiparty computation based on fully homomorphic encryption, design efficient blockchain consensus, and various applications of blockchain.
